Top 5 New Release PC Games

5. Avowed by Obsidian

Genre: Action RPG

Avowed, a brand new release by Obsidian came out on February 18th of this year. As both a chosen Godlike imbued with magical powers and the Envoy of the Emperor your character is granted a downright irresponsible amount of authority to do whatever you please, and you're dispatched to the wild continent of the Living Lands to put an end to a mysterious plague of madness called the Dreamscourge.

4. Doom: Dark Ages by Bethesda

Genre: First Person Shooter

DOOM: The Dark Ages is the prequel to the critically acclaimed DOOM (2016) and DOOM Eternal that tells the epic cinematic origin story of the DOOM Slayer's rage. Players will step into the blood-stained boots of the DOOM Slayer, in this never-before dark and sinister medieval war against Hell.

3. Assassins Creed: Shadows by Ubisoft

Genre: Action-Adventure

Set in 16th-century Japan towards the end of the Sengoku period, the game will focus on the millennia-old struggle between the Assassin Brotherhood, who fight for peace and liberty, and the Templar Order, who desire peace through control, from the perspective of two protagonists: Fujibayashi Naoe, a female shinobi, and Yasuke, an African samurai inspired by the historical figure of the same name. The two characters control differently and provide a unique gameplay style, allowing quests to be approached in multiple ways.

2. Sid Meier's Civilization 7 by Firaxis

Genre: Turn-based Strategy Game

Like other entries in the award-winning Civilization series before it, Sid Meier's Civilization VI challenges you to build an empire to stand the test of time. Playing as one of 20 leaders in the base game with different perks and abilities, you guide your chosen civilization from the Stone Age to the Information Age.

1. Monster Hunter Wilds by CAPCOM

Genre: Action RPG

A wild and unrelenting new frontier. You play as a professional monster hunter, protecting the balance of the ecosystem and hunting monsters. Use the resources you gain from hunts to craft ever more powerful weapons and armor in your quest to unravel the mysteries of the new lands.
